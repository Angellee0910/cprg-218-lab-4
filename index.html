Chinese Cuisine

Delicious Dim sum from China

Spring rolls

Spring rolls are a popular traditional snack in China. They are made of thin dough, rolled with fillings, and fried.

Zongzi is a traditional Chinese rice dish made of glutinous rice stuffed with different fillings and wrapped in bamboo leaves , or sometimes with reed or other large flat leaves. They are cooked by steaming or boiling.

The rice cake is white in color and is made from a mixture of japonica rice and glutinous rice. The more glutinous rice, the softer the product, and the more japonica rice, the product is harder. It can be steamed, fried, sliced and fried or boiled in soup.

Tangyuan is made of glutinous rice. Common fillings include sesame, dates, fruits, fresh meat, vegetables, etc. It is a must-have snack for Chinese people during the Lantern Festival, and it means "reunion".
